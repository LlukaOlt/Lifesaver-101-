Who can perform CPR?
Anyone trained in CPR can perform it, including bystanders in emergencies. Hands-only CPR is an option for those who are untrained.
How deep should chest compressions be?
For adults, compressions should be at least 2 inches deep. For children, about 2 inches, and for infants, 1.5 inches.
