Symptoms of Allergic Reactions

  1. Sneezing.
  2. Itching of the skin, nose, eyes or roof of the mouth.
  3. Runny, stuffy nose.
  4. Tiredness, also called fatigue.
  5. Watery, red or swollen eyes, also called allergic conjunctivitis.
  6. Swelling of the lips, tongue, face or throat.
  7. Vomiting or diarrhea.
  8. Cough, chest tightness, wheezing or shortness of breath.
  9. Anaphylaxis.
  10. Form red or brown patches.
